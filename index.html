Our Mission

To provide children and adults with intellectual disabilities year-round sports training and opportunities for athletic competition in a variety of Olympic-type sports.

Become an Athlete

Special Olympics strives to create a better world by fostering the acceptance and inclusion of all people. Join any of our programs and discover all you can be.

Become a Volunteer

Volunteers are the backbone of the Special Olympics movement. They serve as coaches, trainers, officials, event organizers, fundraisers and managers.

Donation Program

Special Olympics training and competitions take place through the year and every single gift counts. Find out how you can make a difference by giving regularly.
